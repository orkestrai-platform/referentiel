# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive collaboration space.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Focus feedback on content, not people.
- Provide evidence-based reasoning.
- Accept review decisions gracefully.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discrimination.
- Repeated bad-faith arguments.
- Publishing confidential data.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, close, or reject contributions that violate this code.
Serious or repeated violations may lead to contribution restrictions.

For concerns, contact: <security@orkestrai.org>
